About Cend Inc.

Bridging Borders with Every Transaction

Our Mission

At Cend, Inc., our mission is simple: connect immigrants to home; not just through money, but through meaningful, direct support.

Founded with the belief that remittances should create real-time impact, we go beyond traditional money transfers. We empower users to send money or pay directly for goods and services — like school fees, utility bills, and groceries — all from the comfort of their device.

Headquartered in Canada and proudly serving the Ghanaian community, Cend leverages technology to bring speed, transparency, and dignity to cross-border support.

Our Values

Speed

We believe your money should move as fast as your need to help. Our platform delivers instant transfers and real-time service payments.

Security

Every transaction is protected with bank-level security, ensuring your money and personal information stay safe throughout the process.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprises. You'll always know exactly what you're paying and what your recipient will receive.

Real Impact, Real Stories

Every transfer through Cend represents more than just money changing hands. It's a student's school fees paid on time, a family's electricity bill covered, or groceries delivered to a loved one's doorstep.

We're proud to serve the global Ghanaian community, helping bridge the distance between where you are and where your heart is. Our platform doesn't just move money; it moves care, support, and love across borders.

Our Commitment

Based in Waterloo, Canada, our team understands the immigrant experience firsthand. We know what it means to be far from home, and we're dedicated to making your connection to family as seamless as possible.

We're not just a fintech company, we're a bridge between cultures, a support system for families, and a testament to the power of technology to bring people closer together.
